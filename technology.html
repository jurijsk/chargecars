Our EV hardware and software technology has been developed with our selected partners and in-house expertise.

Battery modules

Each battery module contains 204 high nickel content NMC 21700 cylindrical cells, with a total energy of 3.7 kWh - cooled by our bespoke Charge-designed thermal management system and connected by our Flex PCB technology
Powered by 17 battery modules, giving a total energy of 63kWh

Electric motors

Each 100kW permanent magnet brushless AC e-motors producing 380 Nm of torque
Four e-motors, one for each wheel, all independently controlled for intelligent 4-wheel drive that delivers a total power output of 400kW and 1520Nm of torque

Traction inverters

Four 100kW traction inverters convert battery energy to run the e-motors at each wheel.

Unified Computing Platform

All the software modules which run the car’s systems are integrated into an end-to-end computing platform featuring a graphics module and a perception module, UCP runs our two display screens and our advance driving assist systems.
